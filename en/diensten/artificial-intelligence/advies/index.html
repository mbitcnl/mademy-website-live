Our approach

Strategic AI Consulting

The adoption of Artificial Intelligence offers enormous opportunities, but requires careful planning. MadeMy helps organizations at every step — from initial exploration to a fully developed implementation strategy. We are independent and always advise the solution that best fits your situation.
Advisory process

From Discover to Deliver

Our proven 4-phase process takes you step by step from idea to implementation.
  • Discover: inventory of processes, pain points, and stakeholder interviews
  • Define: prioritizing use cases, feasibility analysis, and success criteria
  • Design: architecture, technology selection, and data strategy
  • Deliver: roadmap with milestones, budgeting, and risk analysis
Our vision

Privacy-first & On-premises

At MadeMy, data privacy is central. We always advise solutions where your sensitive data remains within your own infrastructure. This way you easily comply with GDPR and other regulations.
  • On-premises first: your data stays in your own datacenter
  • GDPR-compliant by design, not after the fact
  • No vendor lock-in with cloud AI providers
  • Full control over your AI models and data
