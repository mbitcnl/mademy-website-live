About MadeMy IT Solutions

MadeMy IT Solutions was founded on the belief that organizations deserve to be independent from expensive vendor lock-in constructs. With more than 20 years of experience in IT infrastructure, we help companies implement modern, efficient, and cost-effective solutions.

From MB IT Consulting to MadeMy

Our roots lie with MB IT Consulting, where we have helped organizations with their IT challenges for years. With the rebranding to MadeMy IT Solutions, we strengthen our focus on Cloud Native technologies and open source solutions.
